About Sarsar app

The app aims to allow individuals to sell their materials to students in need, with the condition that the sale takes place within the same school, the materials are in good condition, and the transaction is completed quickly. This unique set of rules eliminates the need for delivery fees, and instead encourages in-person meetups.
